Generation of reports

Glycemic Patterns is an application that recognizes patterns in glucose data collected by blood sugar monitoring devices. Upload your files obtained with your FreeStyle device and download the PDF report to know the patterns of your blood sugar levels.

Customization

The main purpose of Glycemic Patterns application is to help physicians and diabetic patient to identify risk situations or disorders in the amount of glucose in the blood. Decide which information do you want to use for the recognition of patterns and if you want to include additional statistics to go deeper in your historical data.

Machine learning

The application generate a decision tree for each condition (Hyperglycemia, severe hyperglycemia and hypoglycemia) and recognize the patterns that are human-readable and presented in a report. It trusts in decision trees for the pattern recognition and they are included in the report so that you can see where the patterns are coming from
